Research Interests

Current areas of interest

My current research interest is in heterogeneous and monolithic integration of electronic and photonic integrated circuits, with particular focus in high-performance computing and data center applications. My expertise extends across the entire hardware prototype system development with experience in RF/optics simulations, mixed-signal IC design (photonic-electronic circuits) with Cadence-Ansys-Lumerical-IPKISS-Python toolchain with IC package design, PCB design with Allegro and FPGA firmware development with experience in both VHDL and Verilog.
